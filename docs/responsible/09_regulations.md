# AI Regulations

## EU AI Act
The world's first comprehensive AI law.
-   **Unacceptable Risk**: Banned. (Social Scoring, Real-time remote biometric ID in public).
-   **High Risk**: Permitted subject to compliance. (Recruiting, Medical, Critical Infra).
    -   Requires: Governance, Transparency, Human Oversight, Accuracy, Security.
-   **Limited Risk**: Transparency obligations. (Chatbots must say they are machines).
-   **Minimal Risk**: Unregulated. (Spam filters).

## GDPR (Article 22)
-   Right to "not be subject to a decision based solely on automated processing".
-   Right to explanation.

## US NIST AI RMF
Risk Management Framework. Voluntary guidance for US companies.
