# Latent Space & VAEs

## The Manifold Hypothesis
Real data lies on a low-dimensional manifold embedded in high-dimensional space.

## Variational Autoencoder (VAE)
-   **Encoder**: $x \to z$ (Latent).
-   **Decoder**: $z \to x$ (Reconstruction).
-   **KL Divergence**: Forces $z$ to be normal distribution $N(0, 1)$.

## Vector Arithmetic
"King" - "Man" + "Woman" = "Queen".
In GANs/Diffusion: "Photo of person" + "Smile vector" = "Smiling person".
